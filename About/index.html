I am a graduate student at Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) studying in the department of Interior Architecture. I received my BFA from Pratt Institute in Art History. I love to conceptualize new design problems. I am especially fascinated by how design can simultaneously enhance our connections to both tradition and progress.
