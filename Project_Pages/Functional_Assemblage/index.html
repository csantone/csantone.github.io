Functional Assemblage Project

2011
11-22 in.
clay, glaze


The forms for this project were thrown, cut apart, and reassembled. The connections of their parts are hand-built and emphasize the contrast between hand-building, which is naturally more organic, and the more regulated forms which come from wheel-throwing.
