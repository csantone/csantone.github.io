My Places Mask

2010
bristol paper, papier mache, graphite


"What do you want to share about yourself with others?" A mask reveals or hides aspects of a character. This idea of a mask and its role in theater led me to the subject of set design. I created a mask of the sets of some of the places that have most influenced me: my house in Pennsylvania, an apartment in Venice, and the Brooklyn Bridge.
